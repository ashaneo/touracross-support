Support

If you have any questions, issues, or need assistance with the Tour Across app, we're here to help! Please check our frequently asked questions below or contact us directly for further support.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an itinerary?

    You can create a personalized itinerary by entering your preferences in the app's trip planning section. The AI will generate an itinerary tailored to your needs.

  • How do I contact a local guide?

    Go to the 'Local Guides' section in the app, browse through available guides, and click on 'Contact' to get in touch with a guide.

  • What should I do if the app crashes?

    If the app crashes, try restarting your device. If the issue persists, please reach out to us via the contact options below.
